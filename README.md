# 🛒 Blinkit Sales Dashboard | Power BI

An interactive Power BI dashboard built to analyze Blinkit's grocery sales performance across outlet locations, outlet sizes, item categories, and customer ratings.

---

## 📌 Project Overview

This dashboard provides business insights into Blinkit's sales performance by visualizing key metrics such as Total Sales, Average Sales, Product Distribution, Outlet Performance, and Customer Ratings.

The dashboard helps business users identify:

- High-performing outlet locations
- Best-selling product categories
- Sales distribution by outlet size
- Fat content analysis
- Outlet establishment trends
- Product visibility and ratings

## 📌 Business Insights

✔ Tier 3 outlets generated the highest sales.

✔ Regular fat products contribute more revenue than low-fat products.

✔ Fruits and Snacks are among the top-selling categories.

✔ Medium-sized outlets account for the largest share of sales.

✔ Average customer rating remains consistent across outlet types.
